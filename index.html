About me

I conduct research on strengthening the security and resilience of the software supply chain. My work uses empirical software engineering and data-driven methods to understand how reusable software components are developed, maintained, and integrated, particularly focusing on detecting and mitigating vulnerabilities in dependencies.

My research interests include:

  • Software supply chain
  • Software security
  • Mining software repositories
